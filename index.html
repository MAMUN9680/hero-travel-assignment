Popular Tours

Package holidays, popularly known as a tour package or simply the word ‘tour’ in the travel and tourism industry refers either to a package tour escorted or not escorted by the tourist guide.Incidentally, World War II has tremendously developed the package tour concept because of the following reasons:

  • Social and Economic Conditions
  • Lower Cost
  • Increase in Aircrafts
  • Marketing Conditions
  • Legal Requirements
  • Integration took place.

Today, package tours are a vital segment of the world’s travel and tourism industry. According to WTTC, the package tour sales generate $25 billion annually in the United States, $18 billion in Europe, $19 billion in Britain, and $21 billion in Asia. Today tour sales represented 50 percent of all leisure travel sales and 35 percent of all travel agency revenues.

Why Choose Us

There are several advantages to choosing a #tour package over self-planning when it comes to #travel. Here are some compelling reasons why opting for a tour package can be beneficial.

Handpicked Hotels

Time-Saving and Convenience: #Planning a trip requires a significant amount of time and effort. From researching destinations and accommodations to arranging transportation and activities, self-planning can be time-consuming and overwhelming. With a tour package, all the essential arrangements are taken care of by the tour operator, saving you valuable time and ensuring a hassle-free experience.

World Class Service

Cost-Effective: While it may seem like self-planning allows for more control over expenses, tour packages often offer competitive pricing and value for money. Pack The Bag Tours have established relationships with hotels, transportation providers, and attractions, allowing them to negotiate better rates and pass on savings to customers. Additionally, bundled packages often include discounted rates for accommodations, transportation, and activities, helping you save overall.

Best Price Guarantee

Safety and Security: Tour operators prioritize the safety and security of their customers. They carefully select trusted partners, ensure licensed and professional guides, and maintain high standards of safety protocols. In unfamiliar destinations, having the support and guidance of a tour operator can provide an added sense of security and peace of mind.
